Global Youth Environment Essay

I was awarded the Global Youth Environmental Essay Contest for my essay on tackling soil pollution using microbial bioremediation and ESG practices. I highlighted the role of microbes in restoring soil health through biodegradation and synthetic biology, and stressed the importance of combining scientific innovation with ethical governance and community involvement to create a sustainable future.

Johns Hopkins Center for Talented Youth - CTY

I joined CTY Summer Program for a month. There, I learned about forensic science. I solved many cases using forensic science. It was a great opportunity to expand my knowledge of chemistry and biology. I was amazed that I could use science to solve cases that seemed impossible to solve.

Seoul Science Gifted Program

Selected to participate in the Seoul Science Gifted Program from March to November 2023, where I engaged in advanced scientific research and exploration. Gained hands-on experience with scientific concepts, conducted experiments, and collaborated with peers in a challenging academic environment.

Chosun Reporter Training Program

In May 2023, I completed an intensive journalism course where I learned the fundamentals of writing newspaper articles. This experience sharpened my writing skills and gave me insight into the principles of professional journalism.

Science Fair

For one of my experiments, I tested the protein content in various commercial protein drinks to see if their advertised claims were accurate. The experiment involved observing reactions, but one of the necessary materials contained a carcinogenic substance, making it difficult to obtain. I ended up creating the material myself, which led to some errors during the process, ultimately affecting the accuracy of the results. I have written a detailed analysis of the experiment, which is available separately.
